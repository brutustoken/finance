Locking, Microdelegations, Burn Control, and Vote Customization.

Following the latest updates, we have introduced a series of improvements aimed at enhancing the efficiency, flexibility, and transparency in the operations of Brutus Energy Bot.

  • Universal Application of Locking

    • The functionality of locking has been expanded to all orders created, regardless of their duration. We have now streamlined this process by applying locking to all orders uniformly, ensuring that both short-term and long-term orders are subject to the same locking mechanism, thereby promoting a simpler and more consistent user experience. It's worth noting that WOL operations will remain exceptions, allowing users to add energy or bandwidth to existing rentals at no additional cost, offering extra flexibility in the process.

  • Eligibility Optimization

    • In line with our commitment to optimizing the functionality of our bot, we have adjusted the eligibility criteria for providers. We have raised the minimum required energy threshold for eligibility from 25,000 to 32,000 energy units. This adjustment aims to mitigate microdelegations and enhance system efficiency.

  • Introduction of the /burn Command

    • Implementation of the /burn command functionality to provide providers with increased control. Now, providers have the option to decide whether they want to burn TRX to accept new orders when facing bandwidth shortages. Using the syntax /burn yes enables burning. Conversely, employing /burn no rejects burning and only accepts orders with over 600 bandwidth units available, offering flexibility in resource management.

  • Vote for your Super Representative using the /sr Command

    • We have launched the exclusive /sr command for providers, allowing each one to choose their preferred Super Representative (SR) to vote for, with BRUTUS as the default choice. Use the syntax /sr followed by the wallet address of the chosen SR. This option is available for those using the /autofreeze command, which freezes funds daily and casts votes automatically. To enable /autofreeze, users must grant stake 2.0 and vote permissions to the bot. With these additions, we provide more customization and control to your participation in the Tron network.

    With the expansion of locking, eligibility optimization, burn control, and vote customization, we have enhanced every aspect for smoother and more effective operations. Our commitment to innovation and user satisfaction delivers a new level of efficiency in Brutus Energy Bot.
